Apple iPhone 13 128 GB (Blue, 4 GB RAM) Specification

Enjoy technologically advanced features and functions on the Apple iPhone 13 128 GB (Blue, 4 GB RAM) as it is equipped with useful specifications. Available in Blue, the mobile has eye-catching looks. Moreover, the dimensions of the phone measure 146.7 mmx71.5 mmx7.65 mm (height x width x thickness) and weigh approximately 173 grams thus offering a slip-free grip.

The Apple smartphone is powered by Hexa Core (Dual core + Quad core) Apple A15 Bionic processor, which helps the users run numerous files and carry out their work without any interruptions and that too with lightning speed. Moreover, you get 4 GB of RAM and 128 GB of internal storage capacity that allows you to store your files like songs, apps, pictures, vidoes, and more conveniently.

Designed with utmost precision, the Apple iPhone 13 128 GB (Blue, 4 GB RAM) will take your photography skills to the next level.On the rear, the mobile comes with a 12 MP f/1.6 (upto 5x Digital Zoom, upto 2x Optical Zoom), Wide Angle Primary Camera, 12 MP f/2.4, Wide Angle, Ultra-Wide Angle Camera., Along with this, you get 5 x Digital Zoom, 2 x Optical Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus on the rear camera setup that would enable you to click some high quality images and videos. While on the front, the phone sports a 12 MP for making video calls and clicking selfies.

You will be able to see true-to-life color in the visuals while watching videos, photos, or playing games as the phone features an immersive display.

You get various connectivity options on this handset that include 5G supported by device (network not rolled-out in India), 4G (supports Indian bands), 3G, 2G, Wifi Yes, Wi-Fi 802.11, a/ac/ax/b/g/n/n 5GHz, MIMO, Mobile Hotspot, Bluetooth Yes, v5.0, GPS Yes, with A-GPS, Glonass, and more.
